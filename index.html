RULES OF THE GAME

  • Whoever is the youngest player will go first and play as Bender

  • Click on an empty square where you would like to place your peice

  • The first player to connect three in a row vertically, horizontally, or diagonally is the winner

  • Whoever is losing will go first the next game. If players are tied, Bender will go first

  • In case of a game where no player connects three in a row the result is a cats game and Bender will go first upon reset
